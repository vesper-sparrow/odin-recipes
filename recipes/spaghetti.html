Easy Spaghetti Recipe

Description

This plate of spaghetti is so simple and perfect for beginners. It's a quick and easy meal the whole family can enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Sauce
  • Pasta
  • Spices of your choice

Steps

  1. Brown the hamburger in a large pan and drain the fat
  2. Prepare sauce with salt, pepper, tomato sauce and paste, water, sugar, basil, oregano, and garlic
  3. Simmer sauce on low for 45 minutes to an hour
  4. Cook the box of spaghetti noodles as directed on the package
  5. Add cooked noodles to the sauce, and serve right away
